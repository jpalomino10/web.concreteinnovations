Bringing construction expertise to everyone

I started Concrete Innovations. to accomplish one goal and one goal only: Make expertise in the institutional and commercial construction available to anyone tackling a major construction project. Our Concrete Division works under one philosophy, to satisfy client's need of high quality concrete work with three key elements: Loyalty, Hard Work, and Innovation

MISSION....

We live, eat & breathe construction in all its forms in order to lead the industry. Keeping construction going strong in this country is our day-to-day mission.

RESULTS....

We know that you're reputation is only as good as the results of your last job. That's why we proud ourselves on a results-oriented approach.

​METHODOLOGY...

At Concrete Co Innovations Inc., we've taken our collective experience to create our own methodology to keep your project on track and under budget.

OUR COMPAY HISTORY.....

  • 2005 - Concrete Co Innovations Inc. founded
  • 2006 - leading Chick Fill A market of concrete construction around south east completed under budget more than 60 locations.
  • 2007 - Consulted on and Oversaw Construction of new multifamily and Mix used buildings around south east. started to explore Publix supermarkets concrete construction.
  • 2008 Company Grows 75% from previous years due to high quality and under budget. began of our multiple construction of love's travel stations around the south east
  • 2011 - Nominated as One of the Best Commercial Consultants of the year by chamber of commerce committee.
  • 2013- incurred on Posttension design and structural evaluations including restauration.
  • 2014- Joined forces with SCI to cover more clients' necessities and expand our services.
  • 2016- concrete Innovations elected for Georgia Mentor Protégé Program. excellent opportunity to expose our expertise to new clients.
